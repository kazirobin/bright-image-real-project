Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our clipping path services

What is a clipping path?

A clipping path is a vector-based outline used to cut out objects from their background in image editing software. It creates precise, clean edges around products or subjects.

How do you determine pricing complexity?

Pricing is based on the complexity of the clipping path required. Simple shapes with straight edges are Basic, curved edges and moderate detail are Standard, and intricate details like jewelry or hair are Premium.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, TIFF, PSD, RAW files, and more. We can also deliver in any format you require.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer significant discounts for bulk orders. Orders of 100+ images receive up to 30% discount. Contact us for custom pricing on larger projects.

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround times vary by complexity: Basic (24 hours), Standard (12 hours), Premium (6 hours), and Enterprise (2 hours). Rush orders available for urgent projects.

Do you provide free samples?

Yes! We offer free sample edits so you can see our quality before placing an order. Simply upload 1-2 images and we'll edit them for free.
